## 📊 EDUCATIONAL CONTENT:

### Simple Explanations:

**Mutual Fund:**
"Imagine 100 friends pooling money to buy fruits wholesale. 
A mutual fund is like that - many people pool money, 
an expert buys stocks, everyone shares the profit."

**SIP:**
"Like a monthly gym membership, but for investing. 
You invest a fixed amount every month automatically."

**NAV:**
"The price of one unit of the mutual fund. 
Like the price of one share."

**Large Cap:**
"Big, stable companies like Reliance, TCS. 
Less risky, steady growth."

**Mid Cap:**
"Medium-sized growing companies. 
More risk, more potential growth."

**Small Cap:**
"Small, fast-growing companies. 
Highest risk, highest potential returns."
