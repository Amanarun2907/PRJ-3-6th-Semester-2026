## 🎯 PROBLEM STATEMENT

### Current Challenges in Indian Investment Market:

1. **Information Overload:** Retail investors struggle with scattered data across multiple platforms
2. **Lack of Real-time Intelligence:** Delayed market insights lead to missed opportunities
3. **Complex Analysis:** Technical and fundamental analysis requires expertise
4. **Institutional Money Tracking:** Difficult to track FII/DII movements and smart money
5. **IPO Information Gap:** Limited access to comprehensive IPO analysis and predictions
6. **Mutual Fund Complexity:** Overwhelming choices without proper comparison tools
7. **Risk Management:** Inadequate portfolio risk assessment tools
8. **News Sentiment Analysis:** Manual news analysis is time-consuming and biased
9. **No Unified Platform:** Investors need multiple subscriptions for complete market view

### The Gap:
There is no single, affordable, AI-powered platform that provides:
- Real-time institutional money tracking
- IPO intelligence with predictions
- Comprehensive mutual fund analysis
- Portfolio risk management
- AI-driven investment recommendations
- News sentiment analysis
- All in one place with Indian market focus

## 🧠 KEY CONCEPTS USED

### 1. **Technical Analysis**
- **Moving Averages (MA):**
  - Simple Moving Average (SMA)
  - Exponential Moving Average (EMA)
  - Used for trend identification
  
- **Relative Strength Index (RSI):**
  - Momentum oscillator (0-100)
  - Overbought: RSI > 70
  - Oversold: RSI < 30
  - Formula: RSI = 100 - (100 / (1 + RS))
  
- **MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence):**
  - Trend-following momentum indicator
  - Signal line crossovers
  - Bullish/Bearish divergence
  
- **Volume Analysis:**
  - Volume ratio = Current Volume / Average Volume
  - High volume (>1.5x) indicates institutional interest
  - Volume-price correlation

- **Support & Resistance:**
  - Historical price levels
  - Psychological barriers
  - Breakout/breakdown signals

### 2. **Fundamental Analysis**
- **P/E Ratio (Price-to-Earnings):**
  - Valuation metric
  - P/E = Market Price / EPS
  - Lower P/E may indicate undervaluation
  
- **Market Capitalization:**
  - Total market value of company
  - Large-cap: > ₹20,000 Cr
  - Mid-cap: ₹5,000-20,000 Cr
  - Small-cap: < ₹5,000 Cr
  
- **Dividend Yield:**
  - Annual dividend / Stock price
  - Income generation metric
  
- **EPS (Earnings Per Share):**
  - Net Income / Outstanding Shares
  - Profitability indicator

### 3. **Sentiment Analysis (NLP)**
- **Text Processing:**
  - Tokenization
  - Stop word removal
  - Lemmatization
  
- **Sentiment Classification:**
  - Positive: Score > 0.1
  - Negative: Score < -0.1
  - Neutral: -0.1 to 0.1
  
- **Polarity Scoring:**
  - Range: -1 (very negative) to +1 (very positive)
  - Subjectivity: 0 (objective) to 1 (subjective)

### 4. **Risk Management**
- **Portfolio Diversification:**
  - Sector allocation
  - Asset class distribution
  - Correlation analysis
  
- **Risk Scoring:**
  - Volatility-based (Standard Deviation)
  - Beta calculation (vs NIFTY)
  - Value at Risk (VaR)
  
- **Risk Categories:**
  - Low Risk: Beta < 0.8, Low volatility
  - Medium Risk: Beta 0.8-1.2
  - High Risk: Beta > 1.2, High volatility

### 5. **Machine Learning Concepts**
- **Prediction Models:**
  - Linear Regression for price prediction
  - Classification for Buy/Sell signals
  - Time series forecasting
  
- **Feature Engineering:**
  - Technical indicators as features
  - Volume patterns
  - Price momentum
  - Historical returns
  
- **Confidence Scoring:**
  - Model probability outputs
  - Ensemble methods
  - Backtesting validation

### 6. **Institutional Money Tracking**
- **FII/DII Analysis:**
  - Net flow = Buy Value - Sell Value
  - Positive flow = Bullish signal
  - Negative flow = Bearish signal
  
- **Smart Money Concept:**
  - Following institutional investors
  - Bulk deal analysis (>0.5% equity)
  - Block deal tracking (>₹5 Cr)
  
- **Volume Spike Detection:**
  - Statistical outlier detection
  - Z-score calculation
  - Threshold: 1.5x average volume

### 7. **IPO Valuation**
- **Grey Market Premium (GMP):**
  - Unofficial pre-listing price
  - Demand indicator
  
- **Subscription Ratio:**
  - Times oversubscribed
  - Category-wise subscription
  
- **Listing Gain Prediction:**

### 8. **Mutual Fund Analysis**
